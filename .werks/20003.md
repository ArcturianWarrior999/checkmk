# Fix unauthorized users can edit foreign reports

---------- | ---

Users without the permission `Edit foreign Reports` were able to edit foreign reports due to missing permission checks.

### Who is Affected

All enterprise versions are affected.

### Affected Checkmk Versions

* 2.5.0
* 2.4.0
* 2.3.0
* 2.2.0 (EOL)

### Vulnerability Management

We have rated the issue with a CVSS Score of 5.3 MEDIUM (`CVSS:4.0/AV:N/AC:L/AT:N/PR:L/UI:N/VC:N/VI:L/VA:N/SC:N/SI:N/SA:N`) and assigned `CVE-2026-15227`.

We thank Jose Sharon and Pratheesh P. Narayanan (EY GmbH) for reporting this issue.
